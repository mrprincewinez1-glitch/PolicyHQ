Less office badge. More category-defining InsurTech brand.

You were right to push back. PolicyHQ should feel like the command centre for an agent’s book of business, not just another admin tool. Here are five stronger creative territories.

Creative round 02

The current logo has trust and seriousness, but the mark is too dependent on explanation. The strongest version should read fast, own a distinctive symbol, and still look credible when it is only 32 pixels tall.

Recommended territory

01. Renewal Command

A sharp HQ monogram built from a headquarters doorway and a renewal signal. It feels SaaS, operational, premium, and directly connected to the biggest promise: never miss a renewal.
