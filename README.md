# PolicyHQ

> **The operating system for insurance agents in West Africa.**

---

## The Problem

Insurance agents in Ghana and across West Africa manage their entire business manually — policy renewals tracked on paper or in scattered spreadsheets, client follow-ups missed, commissions unrecorded, and WhatsApp messages sent one by one. The result is preventable policy lapses, lost revenue, and no clear picture of business performance.

No software exists that is built specifically for this market, at a price point agents can afford, and designed around how they actually work.

---

## What PolicyHQ Does

PolicyHQ is a SaaS platform that gives insurance agents a single dashboard to run their business professionally and efficiently.

### Core Features (MVP)
| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 🔔 Renewal Alerts Dashboard | Automated alerts at 30, 14, and 7 days before policy expiry |
| 💬 WhatsApp Notifications | Automated renewal reminders and birthday messages sent via Meta Business API |
| 👥 Client Database | Centralised client records with policy history and contact management |
| 📋 Policy Tracker | Real-time visibility of active, expiring, and lapsed policies |
| 💰 Commission Tracker | Monitor earned and projected commissions across all policies |

### Long-Term Roadmap
- Payments integration
- Claims management
- AI-assisted underwriting support
- Multi-agent agency management
- Full insurer operating system for West Africa

---

## Why This Matters

Ghana's insurance penetration rate remains below 2% of GDP — one of the lowest in the world. A significant driver is poor agent productivity and client retention. PolicyHQ directly addresses the operational gaps that cause agents to lose clients and insurers to lose premiums.

The platform is designed for Ghana first, with a clear expansion path across West Africa — a combined market of over 400 million people and a rapidly growing middle class.

---

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Next.js
- **Backend & Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- **Scheduled Functions:** Supabase Edge Functions (renewal trigger logic)
- **Notifications:** Meta WhatsApp Business API, Resend (transactional email)
- **Authentication:** Supabase Auth
